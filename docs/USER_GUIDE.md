## 1. Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the ActionMinutes landing page.
2. Click **Get Started** to create an account.
3. Complete the onboarding flow to set your name, timezone, and preferred AI tone.
4. You'll land in the **Inbox** — your central hub.

### First Steps

- **Add your first task** using the Quick Add button (lightning bolt icon) in the sidebar, or press **Q** on your keyboard.
- **Capture a meeting** by navigating to the Capture page from the sidebar.
- **Create a custom list** by clicking the **+** button next to "Action To Do" in the sidebar.

## 2. Navigation & Layout

### Desktop Sidebar

The sidebar is organized into three collapsible sections. Click the arrow icon next to any section header to expand or collapse it.

| Section | Pages | Purpose |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Action To Do** | Inbox, Custom Lists | Tasks, action items, and your custom lists |
| **AI Assistant** | Capture, Meetings, Calendar | AI-powered meeting tools and scheduling |
| **Action Reflect** | Journal, Notes | Personal reflection and note-taking |

At the bottom of the sidebar you'll find:
- **Actioned** — View completed items
- **Deleted** — View and restore deleted items
- **Settings** — Account and app preferences

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices, a bottom navigation bar provides quick access to key sections: Inbox, Capture, Journal, and Notes.

### Quick Add Button

The lightning bolt button at the top of the sidebar opens the Quick Add dialog for instant task creation from anywhere in the app.

## 3. Inbox

The Inbox is your central command center. It shows all your active items in one unified view.

### What Appears in the Inbox

- **Meeting action items** extracted by AI from your meeting notes
- **Personal reminders** you've created manually
- Items from all your custom lists

### Filtering

Use the filter buttons at the top to show:
- **All** — Everything
- **Meetings** — Only AI-extracted action items from meetings
- **Personal** — Only personal reminders you created

### Interacting with Items

- Click any item to open its detail page
- Each item shows its status badge, due date, and priority
- Items display which meeting or list they belong to

## 4. Quick Add

Press **Q** on your keyboard (when not typing in a text field) or click the lightning bolt icon to open Quick Add.

### What You Can Set

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Task name** | The title of your task (required) |
| **Description** | Optional details |
| **Due date** | When the task is due |
| **Priority** | High, Normal, Low, or None |
| **Tags** | Categorize with labels |
| **Location** | Where the task takes place |
| **Destination** | Where to save: Inbox, Reminders, Meetings, or a Custom List |
| **Repeat** | Daily, Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, or Yearly |

### Tips

- The dialog focuses the task name input automatically so you can start typing immediately.
- Press Enter to save, or Escape to cancel.
- Tags and locations are saved and suggested on future entries.

## 5. AI Meeting Capture

The Capture page is your primary tool for recording meeting information and converting it into structured data.

### Three Ways to Input Meeting Notes

#### 1. Type or Paste Notes
Simply type or paste your meeting notes into the text area. This is the most straightforward method.

#### 2. Upload an Image (OCR)
Click the camera/image icon to upload a photo of handwritten notes or a whiteboard. The AI-powered OCR will extract the text for you.

- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, HEIC
- Works with handwriting, printed text, and whiteboard photos

#### 3. Record Audio
Click the microphone icon to record a meeting in real-time.

- **First time:** You'll see a consent dialog (GDPR compliance). Accept to enable recording.
- **During recording:** You can pause and resume as needed. A timer shows elapsed time.
- **After stopping:** The audio is automatically transcribed and inserted into the notes field.
- Supported formats: WebM, OGG, MP4

#### 4. Upload an Audio File
If you have a pre-recorded audio file, upload it directly for transcription.

### Meeting Details

Expand the "Details" section to add:
- Meeting title (auto-generated with today's date)
- Date and time
- Location
- Attendees (comma-separated names)
- Link to existing empty meetings

### Extracting with AI

Once you have notes, click **Extract with AI** to process them. The AI analyzes your notes and extracts:
- Summary
- Action items (with assigned owners and due dates)
- Decisions made
- Risks identified
- Clarifying questions

You'll be redirected to the Meeting page to review the extraction.

## 6. Meetings & Extraction

### Meetings List

The Meetings page shows all your captured meetings sorted by date, with:
- Meeting title and date
- Number of attendees
- Parse state (draft, extracted, reviewed)

Click any meeting to view its full details.

### Meeting Detail Page

After AI extraction, each meeting page has tabbed sections:

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **Summary** | AI-generated meeting summary |
| **Actions** | Extracted action items with owner, due date, status |
| **Decisions** | Key decisions made during the meeting |
| **Risks** | Identified risks with severity levels |
| **Clarify** | Questions that need follow-up |
| **Highlight** | Key highlights from the discussion |

### Source Card

At the top of the extraction page, a Source card shows linked transcripts with AI summaries and the original meeting notes for reference.

### Working with Action Items

Each action item extracted from a meeting can be:
- **Edited** — Change the text, owner, due date, or status
- **Moved to a custom list** — Organize into your lists
- **Marked as done** — Complete the action
- **Deleted** — Soft-delete (recoverable from Deleted page)

### Template Summaries

For transcripts, choose from **18 professional templates** across 6 categories:
- Standard meeting minutes
- Executive briefing
- Action-focused summary
- Decision log
- Project status report
- And more

Each template generates a formatted, downloadable summary with Export PDF, Print, and Copy options.

## 7. Follow-Up Email Drafts

After extracting a meeting, ActionMinutes can generate follow-up email drafts automatically.

### How It Works

1. From a meeting page, click **Generate Drafts**
2. The AI creates personalized email drafts for each attendee
3. Each draft includes relevant action items and decisions

### Email Integration

If you've connected Gmail or Outlook (Pro plan), you can:
- **Create a Gmail draft** — Opens directly in your Gmail drafts folder
- **Create an Outlook draft** — Opens directly in your Outlook drafts folder
- **Copy to clipboard** — Copy the email body or full email for manual sending

### Draft Management

- Review and edit drafts before sending
- Change the tone to match your communication style
- Track which drafts have been sent

## 8. Calendar

The Calendar page provides a visual overview of your schedule.

### Calendar Integrations

Connect your external calendars:
- **Google Calendar** — Full sync with read/write access
- **Microsoft Outlook** — Full sync with read/write access

### Features

- View events in a calendar layout
- Create local events
- Sync events from connected calendars
- Check free/busy availability
- Export meetings as calendar events (.ics)

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Google** or **Connect Outlook**
3. Authorize access through the OAuth flow

## 9. Personal Reminders

The Reminders page helps you manage personal tasks organized by time buckets.

### Time Buckets

| Bucket | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Today** | Tasks due today |
| **Tomorrow** | Tasks due tomorrow |
| **Next Week** | Tasks due within the next 7 days |
| **Next Month** | Tasks due within the next 30 days |
| **Sometime** | Tasks with no specific due date |

### Creating a Reminder

1. Navigate to the Reminders page
2. Click **Add** in any bucket
3. Enter the task text
4. Set priority, due date, and other details

### Managing Reminders

- **Complete** — Mark as done (moves to Actioned)
- **Edit** — Click to open the detail page
- **Delete** — Soft-delete (recoverable from Deleted page)
- **Move** — Assign to a custom list

## 10. Journal

The Journal is your private space for daily reflection and personal insights.

### Creating an Entry

1. Navigate to the Journal page
2. Click **New Entry** or start typing in today's entry
3. Write freely — your thoughts are private and encrypted

### AI Features

- **Mood Tracking** — Record how you're feeling (Good, Okay, Tough)
- **AI Prompts** — Get thoughtful prompts to guide your reflection
- **Signal Detection** — AI identifies patterns like overwhelm, deadline pressure, conflict, avoidance, or decision-making moments
- **Entry Analysis** — AI can analyze your entry for insights and patterns

### Viewing History

Scroll through past entries to review your reflections over time. Each entry shows the date and your recorded mood.

## 11. Notes

The Notes module provides a secure, feature-rich note-taking experience.

### Security

All note content is encrypted using **AES-256-GCM** encryption at rest. Only you can read your notes.

### Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Tags** | Categorize notes with custom tags |
| **Bi-directional Links** | Link notes to each other; links work both ways |
| **Attachments** | Attach files to notes |
| **Search** | Full-text search across all notes with keyword indexing |
| **Mood** | Track your mood when writing a note |

### Creating a Note

1. Navigate to the Notes page
2. Click **New Note**
3. Enter a title and content
4. Add tags, links, or attachments as needed

## 12. Custom Lists

Custom Lists let you organize tasks and action items into personalized categories.

### Creating a List

1. Click the **+** button next to "Action To Do" in the sidebar
2. Enter a list name
3. Choose an icon from the themed icon picker (Home, Work, Family, Health, Education, Hobby, Wellness, Workout, Social, Personal)
4. Click Create

### Managing List Items

Each list page shows:
- Active items count and completed items count
- Cards for each item with status, due date, owner, and priority
- An inline "Add task" form

### Moving Items Between Lists

From any task or action item's detail page:
1. Click the list/folder dropdown
2. Select a target list, or choose "Inbox" to remove from all lists
3. The item is instantly moved

### Supported Item Types

Custom lists can contain:
- **Personal reminders** — Tasks you created manually
- **Meeting action items** — Actions extracted from meetings by AI

### Editing and Deleting Lists

- Right-click (or use the dropdown menu) on any list in the sidebar to rename or delete it
- Deleting a list removes the list but keeps the items (they return to the Inbox)

## 13. Task Detail Page

Click any task, action item, or reminder to open its detail page.

### Card-Based Layout

The detail page uses a glass-panel card layout with the following sections:

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Task Title** | Editable title at the top |
| **Task Details** | Description, owner, status, tags |
| **Schedule** | Due date (with date picker), duration, deadline, reminder, repeat, priority |
| **Location & Tags** | Where and how the task is categorized |
| **Notes** | Additional notes with attachment support |

### Date Picker

The date picker modal has two tabs:
- **Date Tab** — Quick icons (Today, Tomorrow, +7 days, Next Month), mini calendar, time input, reminder, and repeat options
- **Duration Tab** — Start/end date+time, all-day toggle, and the same reminder/repeat options

### Priority System

| Priority | Visual |
|----------|--------|
| High | Red flag |
| Normal | Amber flag |
| Low | Green flag |
| None | No flag (default) |

### Attachments

Upload files to any task:
- Supported: PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Markdown, Images
- View, download, or remove attachments

### Back Navigation

The detail page shows a dynamic back link that remembers where you came from:
- From a custom list: shows the list name with its icon
- From the Inbox: shows "Back to Inbox"
- From a meeting: shows "Back to Meeting"

## 14. Actioned & Deleted Items

### Actioned Page

The Actioned page shows all items you've marked as complete or done. This gives you a history of your accomplishments.

### Deleted Page

The Deleted page shows items you've removed. From here you can:
- **Restore** — Bring an item back to its original location
- Items show their deleted date and original source

### Soft Delete

When you delete a task or reminder, it's not permanently removed — it's "soft deleted" with a timestamp. This means:
- You can always recover deleted items
- Items stay in the Deleted page until you permanently remove them
